Dozens of truly brilliant and talented people have helped Dopamine come to fruition through fact-checking, editorial assistance, and simple emotional support and encouragement. Many of them, unsurprisingly, have pretty spectacular creative projects of their own. Check out some of their work below!

Agents For ADA

Set in the universe of the Ingress augmented reality game, Daniel Casper et. al. tell the tale of a group of Resistance fighters as they battle for control of their own destinies against the enigmatic Shapers and their collaborators.

How Do You Like Me Now? A True Story of Greed, Fear, and Disaster as a Wall Street Day Trader

For five whiplash-inducing years in the late 1990s, Robert James Perrego worked as a day trader at WorldCo, one of the highest-volume pressure cookers on Wall Street. This short book is a no-holds-barred memoir of greed, passion, heartbreak, and perseverance.

Chiptography

Marjorie Becker goes to chiptacular electronic music shows and takes photos of the DJ and crowd in full swing. It's every bit as much nerd-bopping fun as it sounds. Check out her camerawork, buy her book, and sign her up for your next chip music concert!

7th Sea: Swordsman's Guild

Woe befall the foe that crosses the path of Aaron Chusid! Wielding a rapier in one hand and a pen in the other, Aaron et. al. scribe the sourcebook for the dashing duellers of the 7th Sea game world.

Taliesen Rose

Actress, singer, and voice-over artist — mixing the gumption of Australia with the sophistication and talent of New York City, and bringing it all to sunny San Diego!

Jenny Grace

With beauty rivalled only by her talent, strength, and outspoken eloquence, Jenny Grace seizes the NYC theatre and indie film scenes with tiger-like ferocity. When she's not starring in productions such as Completely Normal, she's writing and producing her own original pieces like Relent: An Indie Musical. And when she's not doing any of that, she's strangling men twice her size at Tiger Schulmann's Mixed Martial Arts gym. Follow her on Twitter!
